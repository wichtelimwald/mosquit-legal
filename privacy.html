MosQuit
Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-24

This policy describes how MosQuit handles your information. The short version: it does not collect any.

Data collection

MosQuit does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no advertisements, and no user accounts. The app has no network access — no data ever leaves your device.

  • No personal data collected
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising
  • No user accounts
  • No network access

Local storage

The only information stored on your device is app preferences such as your chosen theme and language setting. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Permissions

MosQuit does not request access to your microphone, camera, location, contacts, or photos. The app requires no special system permissions beyond basic audio playback.

Background audio

MosQuit may continue playing user-started tones in the background or when the screen is locked. This is intentional: it allows the app to keep running while you use other apps or lock your device. No data is collected during background operation.

Children's privacy

Because MosQuit does not collect any data, there are no special considerations regarding children's privacy. No information is collected from users of any age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be published on this page with an updated date.

Contact
