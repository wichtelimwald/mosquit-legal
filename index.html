MosQuit
Mosquito-free summer nights

MosQuit is a simple, offline iOS utility app that plays specially tuned tones designed to help with mosquitoes, wasps, and other insects outdoors. No sprays, no setup, no tracking — just open the app and let it run.

How it works

MosQuit generates tones through your device's speaker. Place your iPhone or iPad nearby while you're outside and adjust the volume to suit your surroundings. The app works entirely offline — nothing leaves your device.

Effectiveness may vary depending on the environment, device, volume, and insect species. MosQuit is not a substitute for proven protection methods.

Where to use it

  • Patio & garden
  • Balcony
  • Camping & outdoor trips
  • Summer nights
