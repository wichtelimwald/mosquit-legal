MosQuit
Support

MosQuit is a simple, offline iOS utility app that plays specially tuned tones designed to help with mosquitoes, wasps, and other insects outdoors. It runs entirely on your device — no internet connection required.

How to use MosQuit

  1. Open MosQuit on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Press Start.
  3. Place the device nearby when you are outdoors.
  4. Adjust the device volume if needed.

Effectiveness may vary depending on the environment, device, volume, and insect species. MosQuit is not a replacement for proven protection methods such as repellents or protective clothing.

Frequently asked questions

Does MosQuit collect any data?
No. MosQuit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.
Does MosQuit need an internet connection?
No. The app works entirely offline. No network access is required or used.
Why does it keep playing in the background?
MosQuit uses background audio so that user-started tones can continue while the app is in the background or the screen is locked. This lets you lock your phone and put it in your pocket while the app keeps running.
Does MosQuit use the microphone?
No. MosQuit does not request or use the microphone, camera, location, contacts, or photos.
Is effectiveness guaranteed?
No. Results depend on many factors including the environment, device, volume level, and insect species. We make no guarantees about effectiveness.
